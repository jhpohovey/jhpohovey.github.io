Hi all, I'm John. I just completed my first year of graduate school in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), working in the Human Centered Autonomy Lab advised by Professor Katherine Driggs-Campbell.

Generally, my research interests are in Human-Robot Interaction and Human-AI Interaction for social good. I am particularly interested in creating assitive robotic systems for people with impairments, multimodal understanding (e.g., visual language), path planning, and multimodal LLM decision logic.
