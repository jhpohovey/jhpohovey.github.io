About Me

Hi all, I'm John Pohovey. I am a PhD candidate with the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department and Illinois Robotics Group at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I work in the Human Centered Autonomy Lab, advised by Professor Katie Driggs-Campbell. Before this, I obtained my bachelors degrees in {Computer Engineering; Innovation, Leadership, & Engineering Entrepreneurship} also from UIUC.

Generally, my research interests are in Human-Robot Interaction and Human-AI Interaction for social good. I am particularly interested in path planning, multimodal communication, understanding, and decision-making (e.g., visual language, 3D world), for creating assistive robotic systems for people with disabilities.
